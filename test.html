Typography

Normal paragraphs should be gray? Links should be green? H1 should be black, h2 pink and h3 blue and underlined? No problem, everything possible.

Grid

In Flood the grid exists of a few parts: the breakpoints define when what will happeen, a .for-container holds the content section (we are already in one) and centers it. In there could be .for-row, these contain the .for-col-* which seperate the contents. Gutters are already applied to .for-col-* a responsive way.
